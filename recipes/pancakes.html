Pancakes

Description

This easy recipe only needs 5 ingredients and can be made in less than 15 minutes.

Ingredients

    2 Cups Flour
    1 Cup Milk
    1 Large Egg
    1 tsp Salt
    1 tsp Baking Powder

Instructions

    Mix dry ingredients together in a large bowl.
    Mix wet ingredients together in a small bowl.
    Fold ingredients together until just combined, leaving some small lumps.
    Heat skillet with butter.
    Pour 5" rounds of batter into the skillet and cook for 2 minutes on each side.
